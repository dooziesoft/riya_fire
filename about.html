RIYA FIRE & SECURITY SYSTEMS

Our Journey

Riya fire and security systems. was established in 2019 with the commitment to provide safe and cutting-edge Building Management Systems Solutions that will ‘create safer work places’. With a humble beginning out of a garage in Bangalore, with sheer grit and hard work, Riya fire and security systems. today is a pioneer in the field of comprehensive and integrated Security and Surveillance Systems, Fire Protection, Fire Detection and Building Management Systems Solutions. We have a presence across all major Indian cities with offices in Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad and Gulbarga.


Our Story

At Riya fire and security systems we are committed towards resolving issues surrounding fire hazards, unauthorized access, security threats and building evacuation systems with appropriate ‘Personal Protective Equipment’ (PPE). We redefine the way building safety has been practiced in India. Our various protective solutions are designed with cutting-edge technology.
