Water Leak Detection System

Riya fire and security systems provides a variety of professional water leak detection products for early detection, location and reaction to leaks. Whether a data center, IT infrastructure, business or residential property, the Riya fire and security systems water leak detection systems effectively and efficiently safeguard your critical environments. Our state-of-the-art Water Leak Detection System includes a panel with inbuilt software to sense, receive and recognize critical information transmitted by the sensor cables. This system is tamper proof and is designed to sound an alarm in the event it is tampered with or a cable is cut. This system includes an LED display for all zones around the building.
